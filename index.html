Introduction

I am a Ph.D. student of School of Data and Computer Science in Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), a member of joint Ph.D. program between SYSU and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).
My advisors are Dr. Tie-Yan Liu and Prof. Jianhuang Lai.
I have broad interests in Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning and Natural Language Processing.
